Paella

1 Fish Head, 1 bag of Jumbo Shrimp, 3 Cups of Rice, 1 Carrot, 1 Onion, 1 Clove of Garlic, 2 Tomatoes, 1 Bell Pepper, A handful of Green Peas, Scalops, Salt, Pepper, Tumeric, and any other spices.

Follow along the video if you have any questions. Step 1- Chop all the vegetables and clean the shrimp. Step 2- Throw shrimp, onions, tomatoes, bell pepper, carrots, and fish head in boiling water. Step 3- Drain the liquid gold. Step 4- Grill remaining veggies Step 5- Throw liquid gold in skillet with veggies. Step 6- Plate and Enjoy!
