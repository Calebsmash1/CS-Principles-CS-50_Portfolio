Chicken Ice Cream

5 Chicken Heads, 1 Box of Heavy Cream, 1 Box of Sweetened Condensed Milk, Ice Cream Cones(Optional), Sprinkles(Optional).

Follow along the video if you have any question. Step 1- Clean the cow head. Step 2- Grill onions and garlic. Step 3- Cook cow head meat. Step 4- Clean onions and garlic, peel and chop carrots and papaya. Step 5- Throw everything in boiling water in pot. Step 6- Enjoy!
